Travel Insurance Plan

With Easy Travel Family Insurance plan make your family’s trip secured and hassle free. This plan covers people in the age group of 6 months to 70 years. The family plan is devised in three variants: Silver, Bronze and Asian Region, sum insured ranging from USD 25,000 to USD 1,00,000. As a family health plan, it insures two adults (self and spouse) and up to two children under the age of 21 years. Once insured, members can access services like lost luggage / passport assistance, embassy and interpreter referral services, referrals of medical service providers and hospitals, coverage for personal liability and many more.

Personal Accident Plan

You cannot predict accidents and some unfortunate accidental occurrence might bring a huge financial burden to you and your family. Although you cannot guard against all uncertainties of life, you can always take steps towards securing your family’s future and providing financial stability to recover from your financial loss. A Personal Accident Insurance ensures the financial stability for you and your family in the event of an accident.
